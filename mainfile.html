Culture of Nepal

Traditional Temple

Pashupatinath

It is the holiest Hindu shrines in Nepal which is stretched across the capital city of Kathmandu. The temple is the abode of Lord Pashupatinath (an avatar of Lord Shiva)

Traditional Dress

Nawari Attire

A traditional attire that is worn by Nepalies. The Hakku Patashi is worn by the Women of nawari community while the Daruwa Suruwa is worn by the Men of nawari community

Traditional Food

MOMOS

the traditional Nepali momos are steamed dumplings filled with vegetables or meat. The word momo means the process of steaming while making any dishes

Traditional Dance

Chandi Dance

A traditional dance of Rai community. They dance to pray to Lord Shiva and goddess Parvati for the succes, peace and good crops for their village
